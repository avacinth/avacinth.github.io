Valorant is a free-to-play, tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Set in a near-future world, players assume the roles of agents, each with unique abilities, and engage in team-based, strategic gameplay. With its combination of precise gunplay, strategic planning, and hero-like abilities, Valorant offers an intense and competitive gaming experience that challenges players' skills and teamwork.
